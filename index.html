What is RAG?

Retrieval-Augmented Generation combines search with language models

Why Do We Need RAG?

Traditional language models have limitations when answering questions about specific, up-to-date, or domain-specific information. They can:

  • Hallucinate - make up facts that sound plausible but are incorrect
  • Be outdated - not know about recent events or information
  • Lack domain expertise - struggle with specialized knowledge

RAG solves these problems by:

  • Retrieving relevant information from a knowledge base
  • Augmenting the language model with this retrieved context
  • Generating accurate, grounded responses

This creates more reliable, factual, and up-to-date answers.
